Event Details

PROTO-MED is a medical model presentation competition designed to assess creativity, anatomical knowledge, and presentation skills among undergraduate medical students.

Format of Submission
  • Mode: Physical model presentation
  • A one-page summary describing the model to be submitted beforehand
  • Participation as individuals or teams of upto 4 members
Rules & Regulations
  • Only undergraduate students are eligible
  • One entry per Team
  • Plagiarism or the use of pre-assembled commercial models will lead to disqualification
  • Models must visually represent any medical topic
  • Each team must provide a 4-minute oral explanation and a one-page summary describing the model
