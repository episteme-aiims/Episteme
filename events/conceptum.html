Event Details

CONCEPTUM is a research idea presentation competition designed to assess research skills, innovation, and presentation abilities among undergraduate medical students.

Format of Submission
  • Mode: Poster presentation
  • Abstract: PDF Format
  • Font: Times New Roman, Size 12
  • Vancouver style references
Rules & Regulations
  • Only undergraduate students are eligible
  • One entry per participant
  • Plagiarism will lead to disqualification
  • Final presentation as an e-poster
  • 5 minutes for presentation & 2 minutes for Q&A
  • Ethics: Proposals involving human/animal subjects must acknowledge ethical considerations.
  • Abstract (350 words excluding title and references)
  • Title
  • Authors
  • Introduction & Rationale
  • Objectives
  • Proposed Methodology
  • Implications
  • References
