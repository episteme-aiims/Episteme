Event Details

SCHOLARIUM is a research paper presentation competition designed to assess research skills, evidence-based thinking, and presentation skills among undergraduate medical students.

Format of Submission
  • Mode: Oral PowerPoint presentation
  • Abstract: PDF Format
  • Font: Times New Roman, Size 12
  • References: Vancouver Style
Rules & Regulations
  • Only undergraduate students are eligible
  • One entry per participant
  • Plagiarism will lead to disqualification
  • Department & Faculty mentor name required
  • Final presentation as an e-poster
  • 6 minutes for presentation & 2 minutes for Q&A
  • The research must be completed or ongoing (interim results may be displayed).
  • Abstract (350 words excluding title and references)
  • Title
  • Authors
  • Introduction
  • Objectives
  • Methodology & Statistical Analysis
  • Results
  • Discussion
  • References
