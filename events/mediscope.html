Event Details

MEDISCOPE is a clinical case presentation competition designed to assess diagnostic reasoning, evidence-based management, and presentation skills among undergraduate medical students.

Format of Submission
  • Mode: Oral PowerPoint presentation
  • Abstract: PDF Format
  • Font: Times New Roman, Size 12
  • References: Vancouver Style
Rules & Regulations
  • Only undergraduate students are eligible
  • One entry per participant
  • Plagiarism will lead to disqualification
  • Department and faculty mentor name required
  • 6 minutes for presentation & 2 minutes for Q&A
  • All patient data must be anonymized
  • Abstract (300 words excluding title and references)
  • Title
  • Authors
  • Introduction
  • History & Examination
  • Investigations
  • Management
  • Discussion
  • References
